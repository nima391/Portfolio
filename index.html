Who I Am

A short introduction to the person behind the studio's marketing and the community's front desk.

I'm Nimsith Yuthika, a marketing manager working within Sri Lanka's game development scene. For the past two years I've led marketing at Zenthos Productions, where I help shape how our studio and its first title reach players.
